About

A professional plasterer based out of Blaby, all work is carried out under myself ensuring that a high quality finish is delivered to the customer.

Experienced in my Craft

Having worked as a plasterer in Leicester for over 10 years I have worked on various residential and commercial properties where no 2 jobs are the same, I have developed skills to cope and provide solutions to the unexpected. This experience vital in providing a final finished product with no loose ends.

Experience such as turning up to jobs, noticing a slight dampness in the existing plaster and brick work which could easily be overlooked. Picking up on this I am able to advise on the correct course of action ensuring that when the room is plastered it survives the test of time.
